COMPUTER SCIENCE CLUB - VISION & MISSION :

VISION :

To create the most conducive environment for quality academic and research oriented undergraduate education in computer science and engineering and prepare the students for a globalised technological society and orient them towards serving the society.

MISSION :

  • The Club focuses on extracting the hidden talent of the students in the field of Information Technology
  • To improve and enhance the technical skill set of the students by collaborating the club with leading Software Industries
  • To mould students for industry ready expertise by organizing various technical activities
